About Event Finder

Event Finder centralises campus event information so every student can discover opportunities — whether they follow clubs on social media or not. Club members have a simple flow to post events, manage registrations and message attendees.

For Students

Find events by interest, save favourites and get reminders.

For Club Leads

Create events, review registrations and export attendee lists.

For Admins

Moderation tools, analytics and announcements to the entire campus.
